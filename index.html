Hello! I'm Natalie 龍馥瑜.

I am currently a breaking news editor with Bloomberg in Hong Kong, flashing market-moving headlines, writing summaries for the Bloomberg Terminal, charting data and writing longer-form pieces on Hong Kong. Clips here.

Prior to that I graduated from the University of Hong Kong with double majors in Journalism and Computer Science in 2018. I was an intern at the South China Morning Post Graphics Desk and Droste. I also led TecHKU, a technology news site at the university. In summer 2017, I was a visiting student with the Center for Civic Media at the MIT Media Lab, where I helped implement Chinese-language support on Media Cloud and conducted research on Chinese-language media ecosystems.

My passion for technology stems from a curiosity to understand how it affects our lives and how we can manage it for personal entertainment and for social good.

Outside of reporting and coding, I love listening to music and podcasts, and getting involved in tech and journalism communities.
