Deep dive: China in the eyes of the Gray Lady

by Natalie Lung

How this was madeCodeReferences

With a set of articles published by the New York Times from 1995-2017, I set out to explore the semantics of the word 'China' to understand more about:

  1. China's allies in the eyes of U.S. liberal mainstream media, represented by the NYT
  2. China's changing role and involvement in international arena diplomacy
